[1:00 — ARCHITECTURE]
(show assets/architecture_diagram.png)
Let's walk through our multi-agent workflow architecture.
First, every query enters our Security Checkpoint node, which scrubs sensitive PII like emails and phone numbers and blocks malicious prompt injections.
Passed queries are routed to the Lead Business Advisor Agent, which acts as our central coordinator.
The Advisor delegates tasks to the Sales Agent for revenue forecasting, and to the Inventory Agent for stock shortages.
Both agents extract live tables using standard Model Context Protocol, or MCP tools, connected directly to our SQLite database.
Finally, if a major purchase order is recommended, the workflow halts at a Human Approval Pause node, asking the user for authorization before saving the decision to our secure audit logs.
